How to Play

1

The Build Phase

Every round, you are given 5 element markers to place on the grid. Enemies must travel from waypoint 1 to the final waypoint. Use your markers to block their path and force them into a long, winding maze.

2

Merging Elements

If you place or keep three of the exact same element and tier, you can select one and click "Up-Level" to merge them into a single element of the next tier. Higher tiers are much stronger!

3

Crafting Combo Towers

The true power lies in combinations. Check the recipe list on the right side of the screen. If your temporary (unkept) elements fulfill a recipe, the recipe will glow blue. Tap it to immediately "Insta-Craft" a powerful combined tower! Unused materials will turn into stone obstacles.

4

Defend the Maze

Once you've managed your elements, click "START WAVE". Enemies will spawn and attempt to run your maze. As waves progress, enemies will gain nasty buffs like Shields, Dash, Phasing, and disabling Auras. Bosses spawn every 10 waves!

5

The Mystery Shop

Every 5 waves, the Mystery Shop refreshes. Spend the gold you earn from killing enemies to buy global buffs like Za Warudo (slows all enemies), extra building markers, or permanent stat boosts for your favorite towers.
